CA400 Final Year Project

This project aims to provide a way to analyse a news article and determine if it's a source of fake news or not

HOW IT WORKS

Article Extraction

The user inputs the link to the article they want to analyse and the body of that article is extracted. The contents of the article is then used for Text Classification and to check the Similarity score.

Text Classification

With the combination of Term Frequency and Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) and a Classifier, the article's content is classified as 'Reliable' or 'Unreliable'

Text Similarity

Using the title of the target article, a Google search is performed and the top results are collected. A text similarity metric (Cosine Similarity) is then used to individually compare the collected articles to the target article and the similarity score is displayed.
